CTRI

We are building clear, self-study-friendly textbooks for SSCE students.

An initiative of Care for Knowledge

What we’re doing

CTRI is rethinking how textbooks are written—so students can actually learn on their own. We focus on clarity, structure, and alignment between what is taught and what students are expected to understand.

Our Goal

Develop 10 high-quality SSCE textbooks by 2030.

Current Focus

We have started work in the following subjects:

Mathematics Physics Chemistry Biology Agricultural Science

More subjects will be added over time.
